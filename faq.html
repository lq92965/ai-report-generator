285| 286| What types of reports can I generate? 287| 288| 289|
290| You can generate: Daily/Weekly work reports, Meeting summaries, Project status updates, Executive summaries, and more. Simply choose the report type, detail level, and tone — Reportify AI handles the rest. 291|
292|
293| 294|
295| 296| How does the RIE Engine work? 297| 298| 299|
300| RIE (Reportify Intelligence Engine) uses advanced AI to analyze your raw input — meeting notes, bullet points, or free-form text — and automatically organizes it into logical sections with professional headings, bullet points, and executive summaries. It understands context, hierarchy, and the flow of professional communication. 301|
302|

305| 306| What output formats are supported? 307| 308| 309|
310| Reportify AI supports four export formats: Word (.doc) for formal documentation, PPT for presentation-ready slide decks, Email for stakeholder summaries, and Markdown for developers and note-takers. 311|
312|

320| Currently, Reportify AI supports English, Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, Korean, Spanish, German, French, and Portuguese. More languages coming soon. 321|
