Getting Started

What is Reportify AI?
Reportify AI is an AI-powered report generator that transforms your raw meeting notes, chat logs, and quick thoughts into professional Word documents, PPT decks, and email summaries in 30 seconds. It uses our proprietary RIE (Reportify Intelligence Engine) to understand context and structure your content logically.
How do I get started?
Just visit generate.html on our website or download the Android app — no credit card required. You can start with a 7-day Elite Trial for free, no sign-up needed to try the generator.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes! Reportify AI is available on Google Play. The app syncs with your web account, so you can generate and access reports anywhere, anytime.
Do I need an account?
You can try the report generator without an account. To save, export, and access your reports across devices, you'll need to sign up (Google login or email). It's free and takes 30 seconds.

Report Generation

What types of reports can I generate?
You can generate: Daily/Weekly work reports, Meeting summaries, Project status updates, Executive summaries, and more. Simply choose the report type, detail level, and tone — Reportify AI handles the rest.
How does the RIE Engine work?
RIE (Reportify Intelligence Engine) uses advanced AI to analyze your raw input — meeting notes, bullet points, or free-form text — and automatically organizes it into logical sections with professional headings, bullet points, and executive summaries. It understands context, hierarchy, and the flow of professional communication.
What output formats are supported?
Reportify AI supports four export formats: Word (.doc) for formal documentation, PPT for presentation-ready slide decks, Email for stakeholder summaries, and Markdown for developers and note-takers.
What languages does Reportify AI support?
Currently, Reportify AI supports English, Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, Korean, Spanish, German, French, and Portuguese. More languages coming soon.
How long does it take to generate a report?
Most reports generate in under 30 seconds. The speed depends on the detail level you select — "Brief" mode is nearly instant, while "Detailed" mode takes a few extra seconds for comprehensive analysis.
