Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Reportify AI, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website (goreportify.com) and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register via email or Google OAuth, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • User Input Data: We temporarily process the text prompts and notes you submit to generate reports.
  • Usage Data: We collect metadata such as IP addresses, login times, and service usage counts to prevent abuse and improve our platform.
  • Payment Information: All payments are processed securely through PayPal. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our AI generation services.
  • Process transactions and send related administrative emails (via Resend).
  • Manage your account and respond to customer support requests.
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and maintain platform security.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate Reportify AI. These include Google (for authentication and AI processing), MongoDB (for secure database storage), DigitalOcean (for hosting), and PayPal (for payment processing). Your data is shared with them only to the extent necessary to perform these functions.

4. Data Security & AI Processing

Your input data is securely transmitted to our AI providers solely for the purpose of generating your requested document. We do not use your personal notes or reports to train our foundational AI models. We employ industry-standard encryption (JWT, HTTPS, bcrypt) to protect your account data.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or permanently delete your personal information. You can completely erase your account and all associated generated reports at any time by visiting the "Security" section in your Account Hub and clicking "Delete My Account".
